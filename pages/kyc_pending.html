KYC Pending

kyc_pending

Overview

This response template handles verification delays and pending account issues. It's used when customers report that their KYC (Know Your Customer) verification process is stuck or taking longer than expected.

Keywords

verification stuck pending account verification kyc pending vendor account bank account verification

Use Cases

  • Vendor account verification stuck in "Pending" status
  • Bank account verification delays
  • KYC document review taking longer than expected
  • Account activation blocked due to pending verification
  • Customer inquiries about verification status

Common Scenarios

  • New Account Setup: Customer added a new vendor bank account but verification hasn't completed
  • Document Review: Submitted KYC documents but status remains pending
  • Time-Sensitive: Verification delay is blocking business operations
  • Status Inquiry: Customer wants to know why verification is taking so long

Response Template

Template Response

Dear [Customer], thank you for reaching out. Your vendor's account verification is under review. Our KYC team will complete it within 24 hours. If you haven't already, please ensure all required documents are uploaded: (1) Company registration certificate, (2) Tax ID/PAN, (3) Bank statement, and (4) Authorized signatory ID. We'll notify you once verification is complete. Best regards, FinLink Support Team

Response Approach

  1. Acknowledge: Thank the customer and acknowledge their concern
  2. Set Expectations: Provide a clear timeline (24 hours)
  3. Action Items: List required documents to ensure completeness
  4. Reassurance: Promise notification upon completion
  5. Professional Closing: Maintain brand consistency

When to Use

This template is automatically selected by the RAG system when:

  • Query contains keywords like "verification stuck", "pending", "kyc pending"
  • Intent is classified as kyc_verification
  • Urgency is medium to high
  • Customer mentions account verification delays

Team Assignment

Queries matching this template are routed to: KYC Team
