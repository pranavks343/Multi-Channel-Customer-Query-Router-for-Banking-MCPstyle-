Billing Dispute

billing_dispute

Overview

This response template handles billing discrepancies and invoice issues. It's used when customers report incorrect charges, overcharges, or discrepancies in their invoices.

Keywords

invoice charge billing extra charge overcharge monthly invoice discrepancy

Use Cases

  • Incorrect charges on invoices
  • Extra or unexpected charges
  • Invoice discrepancies
  • Billing errors
  • Overcharges or duplicate charges

Common Scenarios

  • Invoice Discrepancy: Customer notices extra charges on monthly invoice
  • Overcharge: Amount charged is higher than expected
  • Wrong Charges: Charges for services not used
  • Billing Error: Mathematical errors in invoice calculation
  • Dispute: Customer disputes specific line items

Response Template

Template Response

Hi [Customer], thanks for bringing this to our attention. We're reviewing your invoice to check the discrepancy you mentioned. Our Compliance team will verify the charges against your transaction records and confirm within 1 business day. If there's a billing error, we'll issue a correction immediately. Please provide the invoice number if you haven't already. Best regards, FinLink Support Team

Response Approach

  1. Acknowledge: Thank customer for reporting the issue
  2. Investigation Promise: Commit to reviewing the invoice
  3. Team Assignment: Route to Compliance team (for disputes)
  4. Timeline: Set expectation (1 business day)
  5. Action Required: Request invoice number for tracking
  6. Resolution Promise: Commit to immediate correction if error found

Important Notes

  • Billing disputes are routed to Compliance Team, not Finance Team
  • This ensures proper investigation and audit trail
  • Disputes require careful verification against transaction records
  • Response time is typically 1 business day for investigation

When to Use

This template is automatically selected by the RAG system when:

  • Query mentions "invoice", "charge", "billing", "discrepancy"
  • Intent is classified as billing_finance with dispute indicators
  • Sentiment is negative (indicates complaint/dispute)
  • Key entities include amounts or invoice numbers

Team Assignment

Queries matching this template are routed to: Compliance Team

Note: Disputes go to Compliance for investigation, not Finance
