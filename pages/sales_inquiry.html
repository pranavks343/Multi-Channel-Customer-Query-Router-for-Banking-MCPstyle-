Sales Inquiry

sales_inquiry

Overview

This response template handles sales and partnership inquiries. It's used when potential customers or partners are interested in products, pricing, demos, or business opportunities.

Keywords

demo pricing enterprise plan partnership white label

Use Cases

  • Product demo requests
  • Pricing inquiries
  • Enterprise plan questions
  • Partnership opportunities
  • White-label solution inquiries

Common Scenarios

  • Demo Request: Want to see product in action
  • Pricing Inquiry: Need pricing information for specific plan
  • Enterprise: Large organization interested in enterprise plan
  • Partnership: Exploring partnership or integration opportunities
  • White Label: Interested in white-label solutions

Response Template

Template Response

Thank you for your interest in FinLink! We'd be happy to discuss how our platform can meet your needs. Our Sales team will reach out within 24 hours to schedule a demo and discuss pricing options tailored to your requirements. In the meantime, you can view our plans at finlink.com/pricing.

Response Approach

  1. Thank: Express appreciation for interest
  2. Engage: Show willingness to help
  3. Team Assignment: Route to Sales team
  4. Timeline: Set expectation (24 hours)
  5. Next Steps: Mention demo and pricing discussion
  6. Self-Service: Provide pricing page link

Sales Process

  • Initial Contact: Acknowledge inquiry within 24 hours
  • Discovery: Understand customer needs and requirements
  • Demo: Schedule product demonstration
  • Pricing: Provide customized pricing based on needs
  • Follow-up: Continue engagement until decision

When to Use

This template is automatically selected by the RAG system when:

  • Query mentions "demo", "pricing", "enterprise plan", "partnership"
  • Intent is classified as sales_inquiry
  • Urgency is low to medium
  • Customer shows interest in products or services

Team Assignment

Queries matching this template are routed to: Sales Team
