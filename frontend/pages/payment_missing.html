Payment Missing

payment_missing

Overview

This response template helps trace missing payments and transfers. It's used when customers report that payments they sent haven't been received or aren't showing in their account balance.

Keywords

payment not received transfer not showing missing payment balance not updated

Use Cases

  • Wire transfers not appearing in account
  • Payments sent but balance not updated
  • Missing transaction records
  • Delayed payment processing
  • Payment trace requests

Common Scenarios

  • Wire Transfer Delay: Large wire transfer sent days ago but not showing
  • Balance Update: Payment processed but account balance unchanged
  • Transaction Missing: Payment sent but no transaction record
  • Processing Delay: Payment taking longer than expected
  • Trace Request: Need to locate a specific payment

Response Template

Template Response

We apologize for any concern. Payment processing can take 2-5 business days depending on the method. Please provide: (1) Transaction reference number, (2) Payment amount, (3) Date of payment, (4) Payment method used. Our Finance team will trace the transaction and update you within 24 hours.

Response Approach

  1. Apologize: Acknowledge customer concern
  2. Set Expectations: Explain processing times (2-5 business days)
  3. Request Information: List required details for tracing
  4. Team Assignment: Route to Finance team
  5. Timeline: Promise update within 24 hours

Required Information

To trace payments effectively, collect:

  • Transaction reference number
  • Payment amount
  • Date of payment
  • Payment method (wire, ACH, card, etc.)

When to Use

This template is automatically selected by the RAG system when:

  • Query mentions "payment not received", "missing payment", "transfer not showing"
  • Intent is classified as billing_finance
  • Urgency is medium to critical
  • Customer mentions payment delays or missing transactions

Team Assignment

Queries matching this template are routed to: Finance Team
